Privacy Policy

Statusly: Status Saver for WA

This policy explains how Darcy Li handles information in connection with the Android application Statusly: Status Saver for WA.

Effective date: May 26, 2026
Intended audience: Adults 18+

This Privacy Policy describes how Darcy Li ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Android application Statusly: Status Saver for WA ("App").

This App is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly market the App to children.

1. Summary

Statusly: Status Saver for WA is designed to help users view, scan, and save WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business status media that has already been viewed by the user and is available locally on the user's device.

The App is designed to process status media locally on the device. We do not sell user data. We do not use the App to read chat messages. We do not intentionally collect personal data for advertising or profiling.

2. Information the App Accesses

Depending on how you use the App, the App may access the following categories of data stored on your device:

  • User-selected file and folder access data
    The App asks you to authorize access to a folder through Android's system file picker. This authorization is used only to locate and read WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business status media stored on your device.
  • Media files
    The App may access image and video files from the WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business status directory after you grant access. The App may create copies of media files in your device gallery when you choose to save them.
  • App configuration and local history
    The App may locally store information such as the folder access permission reference you granted and a local history of saved items to support App functionality.
  • Basic device and app operation data
    The App may temporarily process file metadata such as file name, media type, URI, and modified time in order to display and manage status items inside the App.

3. Information the App Does Not Access

The App is not designed to:

  • read WhatsApp chat messages;
  • read message content from chats;
  • access your contacts list for App functionality;
  • collect precise location data;
  • collect payment data;
  • collect health data;
  • collect microphone or camera recordings through the App;
  • sell personal or sensitive user data.

4. How We Use Information

The App uses the accessed information only for the following purposes:

  • to scan user-viewed WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business status media stored locally on the device;
  • to display image and video previews inside the App;
  • to allow you to save selected media to your device gallery;
  • to maintain locally stored App state, such as folder authorization references and saved history;
  • to troubleshoot and improve core App functionality where permitted by law.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Based on the current App implementation, information processed by the App is intended to remain on the user's device and is not shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

We may disclose information only if required to do so by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

6. Data Storage and Retention

The App is designed to store certain operational data locally on your device, including:

  • the folder access grant reference you choose to provide through Android's system picker; and
  • local saved-item history needed for App features.

Saved media that you choose to download is stored on your device until you delete it.

Local App data remains on your device until:

  • you remove it through App actions, where available;
  • you revoke the App's file access permission through Android system settings;
  • you clear the App's storage; or
  • you uninstall the App.

We do not claim that remote copies of this local data are retained by us because the current App is intended to operate locally on the device.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to help protect information handled by the App. The App uses Android system mechanisms, including the system file picker and platform storage APIs, to limit access to data needed for core functionality.

However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure and reviewing the permissions you grant to the App.

8. Your Choices and Controls

You can control the App's access and stored data in several ways:

  • You may choose whether or not to grant folder access.
  • You may revoke granted access through Android system settings at any time.
  • You may delete saved media from your device gallery at any time.
  • You may clear the App's local storage through Android settings.
  • You may uninstall the App at any time.

9. Third-Party Services

Based on the current App build, the App is not intended to include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or account-based cloud services for core functionality.

If a future version of the App adds third-party services that handle user data, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or when such changes take effect, as required by applicable law and platform policy.

10. International Users

If you use the App in a jurisdiction with specific privacy rights, you may have rights under applicable local law. Because the current App is intended to process data locally on the device, many privacy requests may be addressed by revoking permissions, clearing App data, deleting saved files, or uninstalling the App.
